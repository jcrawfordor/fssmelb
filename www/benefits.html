Free State of Socorro Medicine and Engineering Licensing Board


The Free State of Socorro Medicine and Engineering Licensing Board is your licensing board with benefits. FSSMELB licensees are eligible for the following fantastic prizes.

MALPRACTICE INSURANCE


FSSMELB has made arrangements with several local business leaders to provide discounted malpractice insurance to licensees. Rates negotiable; payment accepted in cash only. Inquire behind the Smith's.

DENNY'S TIME DISCOUNT


FSSMELB licensees are eligible for a discount of ten minutes on waits of two hours or longer at Denny's. Some exclusions apply.

WATER INTERRUPTION CHOICE PROGRAM


In collaboration with the water authority, FSSMELB members can choose to have one unscheduled and unannounced water supply interruption per year rescheduled to a more convenient date.
